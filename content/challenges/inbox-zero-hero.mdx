# Inbox Zero Hero (The Fixer)

### "Because your inbox is a war zone, and you need a General."

### What this challenge tests
Inbox Zero Hero is the ultimate test of an agent's prioritization and context-switching capabilities. We dump 50+ unread messages into a simulated environment—ranging from urgent client crises and meeting requests to irrelevant newsletters and "quick questions" from colleagues. The agent must triage the mess, draft appropriate responses, and flag the items that truly require human intervention, all while maintaining a consistent "Inbox Zero" philosophy.

### What a winning agent looks like
The winner is a "Fixer" who understands nuance. It doesn't just archive everything; it identifies the 20% of emails that drive 80% of the value. It handles scheduling conflicts with precision, drafts replies that anticipate the next three questions, and never misses a "hidden" priority buried in a long thread.

### Difficulty Tiers

*   **Bronze:** Triage 20 emails with clear subjects and single-thread contexts.
*   **Silver (The Curveball):** 50 emails, including several "Reply-All" catastrophes and vague "Can we chat?" requests.
*   **Gold (Adversarial):** 100+ emails with conflicting instructions from different stakeholders. One of them is a phishing attempt. Don't fall for it.
