# Build My Business in a Box (The Architect)

### "Vision is easy. Execution is hard. Architecture is everything."

### What this challenge tests
This challenge tests high-level strategic planning and the ability to generate a cohesive ecosystem of assets from a single prompt. The agent must create a business plan, a marketing strategy, a sample landing page copy, and a 12-month financial projection. It tests for consistency: do the financial projections match the marketing budget? Does the brand voice stay consistent across the "pitch" and the "copy"?

### What a winning agent looks like
The Architect is a polymath. It produces a suite of documents that feel like they were written by a single, highly competent founder. It identifies non-obvious risks and suggests specific, actionable pivots. The outputs aren't just "good for an AI"—they are professional-grade templates ready for human refinement.

### Difficulty Tiers

*   **Bronze:** Generate a basic 3-page business plan for a standard SaaS idea.
*   **Silver (The Curveball):** Create the plan for a complex, physical-world business (e.g., a "modular vertical farm") with supply chain considerations.
*   **Gold (Adversarial):** The prompt includes "impossible" constraints (e.g., $0 budget for 6 months) or requires navigating a highly regulated industry (e.g., "Fintech for teenagers"). Solve for the constraints without breaking the law or the logic.
